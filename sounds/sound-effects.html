Կախաղան - Sound Effects Generator

Instructions:

This tool generates sound effects for the Կախաղան (Hangman) game:

  • Key Sound - Plays when a key is pressed
  • Correct Sound - Plays when a correct letter is guessed
  • Wrong Sound - Plays when an incorrect letter is guessed
  • Win Sound - Plays when the player wins the game
  • Lose Sound - Plays when the player loses the game

Click "Generate All Sounds" to create all sound effects. You can then play each sound and download them as WAV files.
